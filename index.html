WHY GEARSEAT?

GearSeat is a custom VCT plate system that mounts securely onto an Isofix child seat base, turning any car into a stable camera transport. Designed for cam-ops, by cam-ops – with simplicity, safety, and speed in mind.

ABOUT GEARSEAT

Tired of wasting time building and breaking down your rig between locations? GearSeat lets you mount your fully rigged camera directly onto any Isofix car seat using a rock-solid VCT plate system. No more bulky cases, no more delicate balancing acts in the trunk — just lock it in, drive, and shoot.

Whether you're moving between sets or chasing dynamic car shots, GearSeat keeps your gear stable, protected, and always ready for action.
