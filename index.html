WHY GEARSEAT?

GearSeat is a custom VCT based system that mounts securely onto Isofix in your vehicle.
No more need to take apart your rig. Gearseat makes moving around with your gear quicker and easier!
Designed FOR cam-ops, BY cam-ops – with simplicity, safety, and speed in mind.

ABOUT GEARSEAT

Tired of wasting time building and breaking down your rig between locations? GearSeat lets you mount your fully rigged camera directly into any car with Isofix using a rock-solid VCT plate system. No more bulky cases, no more camera bags that are simply just too small, no more stacking your delecate gear on top of eachother in the trunk — just lock it in, drive, and shoot.

Whether you're moving between sets or keeping your gear secure for a long drive, GearSeat keeps your gear protected and always ready for action.
