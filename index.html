WHAT IS THE GEARSEAT?

The Gearseat is a smart, secure solution for mounting your camera rig inside any car with ISOFIX. Built for cameramen, by cameramen.

The Gearseat let's you mount your fully rigged camera directly into the back seat, no more dismantling, no more bulky cases, and no more gear stacked dangerously in the trunk. Just lock it in, drive, and shoot.

Whether you’re hopping between locations or transporting your setup over long distances, The Gearseat keeps your camera safe, secure, and always ready for action, with speed, simplicity, and safety in mind.

THE PLAN

At this moment, The Gearseat is in the prototype phase. We're testing, refining, and rethinking every detail, from the ISOFIX connection to the camera mount, to ensure a solid, safe and user-friendly solution for filmmakers on the move.

Our goal?
To create the most reliable way to mount a camera inside a car. No tools, no hassle, just click & shoot.

We're working closely with professionals in the field to make sure The Gearseat fits real-world demands. This means:

  • Fast and secure camera placement
  • Compatible with ISOFIX systems
  • Designed for Sony VCT-14, Arri Dovetail and other baseplates
  • Build like a tank

We’re not rushing it.
We believe in building it right, not just fast.
